Meet the Owner

Owner Solomon Sarhadi started Gyros & More back in 2008, after spending 21 years working for the fast food giant Carl's Jr. After over two decades of corporate service, Sarhadi decided it was time to be his own boss. Gyros & More specializes in Mediterranean and Middle Eastern food. The crowd favorite is the classic gyro. Served with either lamb or chicken, the gyro sandwich is a collection of meat, tomatoes, lettuce, onions, creamy tzatziki sauce all served on fresh pita bread. Along with gyros, the restaurant serves up kabobs, falafel, sandwiches and salads .
